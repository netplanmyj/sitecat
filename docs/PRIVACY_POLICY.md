# Privacy Policy for SiteCat

**Last Updated: November 21, 2025**

## Introduction

SiteCat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address and name when you sign in with Google
- **Website Data**: URLs and configuration of websites you choose to monitor
- **Monitoring Results**: Status check results and link scan data

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: App interaction patterns and feature usage
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Authentication Data**: Google Sign-In tokens (managed by Firebase Authentication)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Provision**: Monitoring websites and detecting broken links
- **Account Management**: User authentication and data synchronization
- **Service Improvement**: Understanding usage patterns to enhance features
- **Security**: Protecting against unauthorized access

## Data Storage and Security

### Cloud Storage

- Your data is stored in **Google Cloud Firestore** (Google Cloud Platform)
- Data centers are located in: `us-central1` (United States)
- We implement industry-standard security measures

### Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Monitoring history: Automatically cleaned up after 30 days (configurable)
- Deleted accounts: All associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google Cloud)

- **Firebase Authentication**: For Google Sign-In
- **Cloud Firestore**: For data storage and synchronization
- **Firebase Cloud Functions**: For background monitoring (future feature)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We may share information only when:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your monitoring data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@netplan.co.jp

## Children's Privacy

SiteCat is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to your registered address
