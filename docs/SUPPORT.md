## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

1. Download SiteCat from the App Store
2. Tap "Sign in with Google"
3. Authorize the app to access your Google account

### 2. Add Your First Website

1. Tap the "+" button in the Sites tab
2. Enter your website URL (e.g., https://example.com)
3. Give your site a name
4. Tap "Save"

### 3. Monitor Your Website

#### Quick Check
- Tap "Quick Check" to verify your website is accessible
- View response time and HTTP status

#### Full Scan
- Tap "Full Scan" to check for broken links
- Monitor progress in real-time
- View detailed results in the Results tab

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is SiteCat free?**  
A: Yes, the current version is completely free with all features available.

**Q: What platforms are supported?**  
A: SiteCat is available for iOS and Android devices.

**Q: Do I need an account?**  
A: Yes, a Google account is required for data synchronization and cloud storage.

### Monitoring

**Q: How often should I check my website?**  
A: It depends on your needs. For critical sites, check daily. For less critical sites, weekly checks may suffice.

**Q: What does "Quick Check" do?**  
A: Quick Check verifies that your website's homepage is accessible and returns the correct status code.

**Q: What does "Full Scan" do?**  
A: Full Scan crawls your entire website to find broken links, checking all internal pages and links.

**Q: How long does a Full Scan take?**  
A: It depends on your website size. Small sites (10-20 pages) take a few seconds, while larger sites may take several minutes.

**Q: Can I stop a scan in progress?**  
A: Yes, tap the "Pause" button during a scan. You can resume later or cancel it.

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: Your data is securely stored in Google Cloud Firestore (US region).

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**  
A: Yes, data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption.

**Q: How long is monitoring history kept?**  
A: By default, monitoring history is kept for 30 days. Older data is automatically deleted.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Contact support@netplan.co.jp for data export requests.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Go to Profile tab ‚Üí Account Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app shows my website is down, but it's accessible in my browser.**  
A: This can happen if:
- Your website blocks automated requests
- There's a temporary network issue
- Your website uses specific authentication

Try checking again in a few minutes. If the problem persists, contact support.

**Q: Full Scan is stuck or very slow.**  
A: Large websites may take time to scan. You can:
- Pause and resume the scan
- Check your internet connection
- Try again during off-peak hours

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications.**  
A: Automatic notifications are a planned feature for the premium version. Currently, you need to manually check your sites.

**Q: The app crashes or freezes.**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Force close and restart the app
2. Check for app updates in the App Store
3. Restart your device
4. If the problem persists, contact support with details

### Account & Billing

**Q: Will there be a premium version?**  
A: We plan to introduce premium features in the future, including:
- Automatic monitoring
- Push notifications
- Advanced reporting
- Priority support

**Q: How do I change my email address?**  
A: Your email is tied to your Google account. Update it in your Google account settings.

**Q: I signed in with the wrong Google account.**  
A: Sign out from Profile ‚Üí Sign Out, then sign in with the correct account.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Sign In

1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
2. Check that Google Sign-In is not blocked
3. Try signing out of your Google account in your device settings and signing back in
4. Reinstall the app if the problem persists

### Sites Not Loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify the website URL is correct
3. Ensure the website is actually accessible
4. Try removing and re-adding the site

### Sync Issues

1. Check internet connection
2. Sign out and sign back in
3. Force close and restart the app
4. Check that you're using the same Google account on all devices
